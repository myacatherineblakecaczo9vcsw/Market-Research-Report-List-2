<p><p>Keylock switches are locking switches that require a key to operate, providing security and preventing unauthorized use. Single-pole keylock switches have one set of contacts, while double-pole keylock switches have two sets. Single-pole keylock switches are commonly used for basic on/off control, while double-pole keylock switches are often used for more complex control applications requiring separate circuits to be switched. Both types of keylock switches offer varying levels of security and can be found in a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
