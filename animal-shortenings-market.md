<p><p>Animal shortenings are commonly available in four main market types: solid, liquid, cake/icing, and all-purpose. </p><p>Solid animal shortenings are typically used for baking and frying, providing a stable texture. Liquid shortenings are most commonly used for deep frying due to their higher smoke point. Cake/icing shortenings are specially formulated for use in baking for a light and airy texture. All-purpose shortenings can be used in a variety of cooking and baking applications. There are also other specialty animal shortenings available for specific uses.</p></p>
