<p><strong>The Multilayer Co-fired Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LTCC Substrate</li><li>HTCC Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multilayer Co-fired Substrates refer to ceramic substrates that have multiple layers of conductive traces buried within the ceramic layers. LTCC (Low Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) Substrates utilize a low firing temperature process, making them suitable for applications requiring lower thermal expansion coefficients. HTCC (High Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) Substrates, on the other hand, are fired at higher temperatures, providing them with higher thermal conductivity and better mechanical properties. Both types of substrates offer different advantages and are used in various applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
