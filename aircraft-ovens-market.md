<p><p>Aircraft ovens come in various types including electric ovens, conduction ovens, convection ovens, and others. Electric ovens use electrical heating elements to cook food inside the oven. Conduction ovens rely on direct heat transfer from a heating element to cook food evenly. Convection ovens utilize a fan to circulate hot air evenly throughout the oven for faster and more uniform cooking. Other types of aircraft ovens may include microwave ovens or combination ovens with multiple cooking methods.</p></p>
