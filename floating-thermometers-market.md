<p><p>Floating thermometers come in two main types: digital floating thermometers and graduated floating thermometers. Digital floating thermometers are equipped with electronic sensors that provide accurate temperature readings instantly. They are popular among users who prioritize convenience and precision. On the other hand, graduated floating thermometers have a traditional design with a glass tube and mercury or alcohol filling. They are commonly used in swimming pools and aquariums for monitoring water temperature. Both types cater to different consumer preferences in the market for floating thermometers.</p></p>
