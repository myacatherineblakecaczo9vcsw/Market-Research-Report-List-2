<p><p>Silicone injection machine market types include thermoplastic material, thermoset plastic, silica gel, rubber, and die casting. Thermoplastic material is known for its ability to soften when heated and harden when cooled, making it ideal for injection molding processes. Thermoset plastic, on the other hand, undergoes a chemical reaction when heated, resulting in a permanent shape. Silica gel is a versatile material with high thermal stability, while rubber offers flexibility and durability. Die casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold cavity to create complex shapes efficiently.</p></p>
