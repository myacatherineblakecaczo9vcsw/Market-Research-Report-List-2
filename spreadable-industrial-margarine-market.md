<p><p>Spreadable industrial margarine can be categorized into two main market types: hard and soft. Hard spreadable margarine has a higher fat content and is commonly used in baking and cooking applications. It is solid at room temperature and provides a rich flavor to dishes. Soft spreadable margarine, on the other hand, has a lower fat content and is often used as a spread on bread, toast, and other baked goods. It is soft and easily spreadable straight from the refrigerator. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and usage needs in the food industry.</p></p>
