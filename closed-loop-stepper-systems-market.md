Permanent Magnet Closed Loop Stepper Systems utilize permanent magnets in the rotor, Hybrid Closed Loop Stepper Systems incorporate both permanent magnets and variable reluctance in the rotor, and Variable Reluctance Closed Loop Stepper Systems use only variable reluctance for the rotor.
