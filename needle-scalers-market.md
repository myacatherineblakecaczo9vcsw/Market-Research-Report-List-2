<p><p>Needle scalers are commonly available in two main types: straight and pistol grip. Straight needle scalers have a long, straight handle that allows for better reach in tight spaces and more precise control. On the other hand, pistol grip needle scalers have a handle that is perpendicular to the needle assembly, providing a more ergonomic and comfortable grip for extended use. Both types of needle scalers are widely used in industries such as metal fabrication, construction, and automotive for removing rust, paint, and other coatings from surfaces.</p></p>
