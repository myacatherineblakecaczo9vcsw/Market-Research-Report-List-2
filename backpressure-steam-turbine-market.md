<p><p>Backpressure steam turbines are categorized into two main market types: single-stage and multistage turbines. Single-stage turbines are designed with only one set of nozzles and blades, making them simpler and more compact. They are commonly used in smaller industrial applications where a constant pressure steam supply is available. In contrast, multistage turbines have multiple sets of nozzles and blades, allowing for higher efficiency and power output. These turbines are typically used in larger industrial facilities that require varying steam pressures and temperatures. Both types of turbines play a crucial role in generating electricity and mechanical power from steam.</p></p>
