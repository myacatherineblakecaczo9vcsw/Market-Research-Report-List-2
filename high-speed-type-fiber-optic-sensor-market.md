<p><p>High Speed Type Fiber Optic Sensor Market includes two main types of fiber optic cables, Single-Mode Fiber and Multimode Fiber. Single-Mode Fiber optic cables are designed for long-distance transmission with a single light path, ideal for high-speed data transmission over long distances. Multimode Fiber optic cables have multiple light paths, making them suitable for shorter distance transmissions at high speeds within data centers and local area networks. Both types of cables play a crucial role in enabling high-speed data transfer in various industries such as telecommunications and networking.</p></p>
