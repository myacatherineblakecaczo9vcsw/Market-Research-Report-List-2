<p><p>A Deliquescent Dryer is a kind of industrial dryer used for removing moisture from compressed air or gas streams. Deliquescent dryers are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and oil & gas.</p><p>The Deliquescent Dryer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period. <p><p>Deliquescent dryers can be classified into two main types based on their loading mechanism: top loading and front loading. Top loading dryers have a lid on the top of the unit that opens to load and unload the desiccant material. On the other hand, front loading dryers have a door on the front of the unit for easy access to the desiccant material. Both types of dryers are commonly used in industrial settings for removing moisture from compressed air or gas streams.</p></p>
