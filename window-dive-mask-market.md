<p><p>Full window dive masks have a single large window that covers the entire front of the mask, providing a wide field of vision and maximizing visibility underwater. Half window dive masks, on the other hand, have a split window design with two separate lenses, offering better depth perception and allowing for easier equalization. Both types of masks are popular among divers, with full window masks preferred for panoramic views and half window masks favored for comfort and practicality.</p></p>
