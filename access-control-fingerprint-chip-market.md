<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Access control fingerprint chips are commonly divided into two types: Capacitive Fingerprint Module and Optical Fingerprint Module. Capacitive modules use electrical signals to detect fingerprint patterns, offering high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, Optical modules use light to capture fingerprint images, providing a fast and efficient way to verify identities. Both types are widely used in access control systems for their security features and ease of use in various applications.</p></p>
