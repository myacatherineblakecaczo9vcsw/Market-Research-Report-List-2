<p><p>Stair stepper machines are categorized into two main types: revolving stairs and steppers. Revolving stair machines consist of a continuously moving set of stairs, simulating a real staircase climbing experience. Users must keep up with the pace of the revolving stairs. On the other hand, stepper machines involve individual steps that move up and down, providing a more controlled and customizable workout. Both types offer cardiovascular benefits and help strengthen leg muscles, but they provide slightly different workout experiences.</p></p>
