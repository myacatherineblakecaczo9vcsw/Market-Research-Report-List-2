<p><p>Waterproof luminaires are classified based on their level of protection against water and dust ingress, represented by IP ratings such as IP65, IP67, and IP68. IP65 luminaires are protected against low-pressure water jets, IP67 against temporary immersion in water, and IP68 against continuous immersion in water. These ratings indicate the suitability of the luminaires for different environments, helping consumers choose the right product for their specific needs. Each IP rating ensures varying levels of protection against water and dust, providing durability and safety.</p></p>
