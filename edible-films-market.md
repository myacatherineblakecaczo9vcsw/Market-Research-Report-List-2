<p><ul><li>Protein</li><li>Polysaccharides</li><li>Lipids</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edible films can be categorized into four main types based on their composition: Protein, Polysaccharides, Lipids, and Others. Protein-based edible films are typically derived from sources such as whey, gelatin, and soy protein. Polysaccharide-based films are made from substances like starch, cellulose, and chitosan. Lipid-based films are created using fats and oils. The Others category encompasses edible films made from various other ingredients, such as gums, pectins, and composite materials. Each type of edible film offers unique characteristics and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
