<p><p>Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and Other hydro turbine runners are types of turbines used in hydroelectric power plants to convert water flow into mechanical energy. The Francis runner is widely used for medium to high head applications, while the Kaplan runner is more efficient for low head and high flow conditions. The Pelton runner is ideal for high head and low flow situations. Other types of runners include crossflow and tubular turbines, each designed for specific hydropower generation requirements.</p></p>
