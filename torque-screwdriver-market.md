Electric torque screwdrivers are powered by electricity and provide consistent torque output for repetitive tasks in industries. They are suitable for high precision applications and offer adjustable torque settings. On the other hand, manual torque screwdrivers rely on physical force applied by the operator. They are versatile, portable, and cost-effective tools commonly used in maintenance and repair work where precision is not a critical factor.</p></p>
