<p><strong>The Metal Neurovascular Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carotid artery stents</li><li>Intracranial stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal neurovascular stents are used to treat blockages or narrowings in blood vessels of the brain. Carotid artery stents are specifically designed for the carotid arteries, which supply blood to the brain, while intracranial stents are used within the brain itself. Carotid artery stents help prevent strokes by improving blood flow, while intracranial stents are used to treat aneurysms or other vascular abnormalities in the brain. Both types of stents are made of metal and are inserted into the affected blood vessel during a minimally invasive procedure.</p></p>
