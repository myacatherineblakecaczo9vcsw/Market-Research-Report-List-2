<p><p>Quick connect couplings are commonly divided into two types: manual type and automatic type. Manual type quick connect couplings require physical manipulation to connect and disconnect the coupling, providing users with more control over the process. On the other hand, automatic type quick connect couplings feature self-locking mechanisms that allow for quick and easy connection without the need for manual intervention. Both types cater to users' preferences for convenience, efficiency, and ease of use in various industrial applications.</p></p>
