<p><p>Electro Pneumatic Positioners in the market are available in two main types, Single Acting and Double Acting. </p><p>Single Acting type positioners are controlled by air pressure acting in only one direction, typically used for simple on/off applications. </p><p>Double Acting type positioners are controlled by air pressure acting in both directions, offering more precise control and flexibility for various applications. </p><p>Both types of positioners play a crucial role in controlling the position of pneumatic valves in industrial processes, thereby ensuring efficient operation and performance.</p></p>
