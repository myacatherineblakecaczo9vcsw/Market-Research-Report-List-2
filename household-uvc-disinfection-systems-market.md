<p><p>Household UVC disinfection systems encompass two main types: air & surface disinfection systems and boxes & cabinets disinfection systems. Air & surface disinfection systems utilize UVC light to eliminate pathogens suspended in the air as well as on surfaces within a room. On the other hand, boxes & cabinets disinfection systems are designed to disinfect smaller items such as keys, phones, and other personal belongings by placing them inside a UV light-enclosed container. These systems provide an effective method for maintaining a clean and sanitary home environment.</p></p>
