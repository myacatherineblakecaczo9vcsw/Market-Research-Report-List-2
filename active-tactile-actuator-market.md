<p><p>Active Tactile Actuators are devices that provide tactile feedback through vibration or motion. Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators use a small motor with an unbalanced weight to create vibrations. Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA) use a magnetic coil and spring system to produce precise vibrations. Other types of actuators include piezoelectric actuators, electroactive polymer actuators, and shape memory alloy actuators. Each type of actuator has unique characteristics and applications in industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
