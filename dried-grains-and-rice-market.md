<p><p>Dried grains and rice market types include rough (unmilled) rice, parboiled rice, brown rice, fully milled (white) rice, dried beans, chickpeas, and other varieties. Rough rice is the whole grain with the outer husk intact. Parboiled rice is partially boiled in the husk before milling, retaining more nutrients. Brown rice is unmilled but with only the husk removed. Fully milled (white) rice has the husk, bran, and germ removed. Dried beans, chickpeas, and other varieties offer a variety of nutritional profiles and cooking options.</p></p>
