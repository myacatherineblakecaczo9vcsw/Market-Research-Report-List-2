<p><p>Dry magnetic separators are classified into two major categories based on the strength of the magnetic field they generate: weak magnetic field and strong magnetic field separators. Weak magnetic field separators are designed for the separation of fine particles and typically operate at lower magnetic field intensities. On the other hand, strong magnetic field separators are used for the efficient separation of coarse particles and operate at higher magnetic field intensities. Both types of separators are essential for different applications in industries such as mining, recycling, and construction.</p></p>
