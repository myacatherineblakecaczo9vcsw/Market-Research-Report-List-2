<p><p>The pet food ingredients market can be segmented into four main types: animal derivatives, plant derivatives, additives, and others. Animal derivatives include ingredients such as meat, poultry, and fish, which are sourced from animals. Plant derivatives refer to ingredients like grains, fruits, and vegetables that are sourced from plants. Additives are additional substances added to pet food for nutritional enhancement or preservation. The 'other' market includes ingredients like vitamins, minerals, and preservatives that do not fall into the other three categories.</p></p>
