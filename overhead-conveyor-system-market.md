<p><p>An overhead conveyor system is a type of material handling equipment used to transport goods or materials from one location to another in a manufacturing or distribution facility. These systems typically consist of a track system mounted overhead, along with carriers or trolleys that move along the track to transport items. Overhead conveyor systems are commonly used in industries such as automotive, food and beverage, aerospace, and retail.</p><p>The global overhead conveyor system market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 14.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to factors such as increasing demand for automation in manufacturing processes, rising adoption of overhead conveyor systems in various industries, and advancements in technology.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the overhead conveyor system market is the development of smart conveyor systems that are equipped with sensors and software to optimize efficiency and improve productivity. These systems can track and monitor the movement of goods, identify bottlenecks in the production process, and provide real-time data for better decision-making. Additionally, the integration of conveyor systems with other automation technologies such as robotics and artificial intelligence is expected to further drive market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
