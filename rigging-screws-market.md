<p><p>Rigging screws are essential components used in various applications such as sailing, construction, and lifting. There are two main types of rigging screws based on their design: open-body and closed-body. Open-body rigging screws have a more traditional design where the body is formed of separate parts that can be unscrewed for easy access to the threads. Closed-body rigging screws, on the other hand, have a solid body that provides more protection and security for the threads, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
