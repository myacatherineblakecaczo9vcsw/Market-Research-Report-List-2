<p><p>Mango Seed Oil and Butter come in two main market types: Cold-Pressed and Expeller-Pressed. Cold-pressed products are extracted from the seeds using minimal heat, which helps maintain their nutritional content and beneficial properties. This method is preferred by those seeking a more natural and pure product. Expeller-pressed products, on the other hand, are extracted using mechanical force, which may involve higher temperatures. While still retaining some nutrients, expeller-pressed products are often chosen for their efficiency and affordability.</p></p>
