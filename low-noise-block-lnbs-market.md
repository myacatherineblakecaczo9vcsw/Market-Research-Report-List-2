<p><p>LNBs are used in satellite dishes to receive and amplify satellite signals. There are different types of LNBs designed for different frequency bands such as C-Band, Ku-Band, Ka-Band, and X-Band. C-Band LNBs operate at lower frequencies and are mainly used for commercial satellite communications. Ku-Band LNBs operate at higher frequencies and are commonly used for satellite TV broadcasting. Ka-Band LNBs are designed for higher data rate transmissions, while X-Band LNBs are used for military and government applications requiring secure and reliable communications.</p></p>
