<p><strong>The Variable Air Volume Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Duct VAV</li><li>Dual Duct VAV</li><li>Induction VAV</li><li>Fan Powered VAV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Air Volume Systems are used for efficient heating, ventilation, and air conditioning in buildings. Single Duct VAV systems have one duct for supply air, while Dual Duct VAV systems have separate ducts for hot and cold air. Induction VAV systems use mixing boxes to regulate airflow. Fan Powered VAV systems use a fan to boost airflow in individual zones. These different types cater to various building requirements and offer flexibility in controlling air volume and temperature for optimal comfort and energy savings.</p></p>
