<p><p>Substrate Support Mat is available in two main types - Intumescent and Non-Intumescent. Intumescent Substrate Support Mat is designed to expand and create a protective barrier in the event of a fire, helping to prevent the spread of flames. On the other hand, Non-Intumescent Substrate Support Mat does not expand but still provides support and protection to the substrate. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for fire protection and insulation purposes.</p></p>
