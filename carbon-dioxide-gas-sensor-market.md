<p><p>Carbon Dioxide Gas Sensors are available in three main types: NDIR, Catalytic, and Thermal Conductivity. NDIR CO2 Gas Sensors use infrared technology to accurately measure CO2 levels. Catalytic CO2 Gas Sensors detect CO2 through a chemical reaction. Thermal Conductivity CO2 Gas Sensors measure changes in thermal conductivity due to CO2 presence. Each type of sensor has its unique advantages and is used in various applications such as air quality monitoring, HVAC systems, industrial processes, and indoor air quality control.</p></p>
