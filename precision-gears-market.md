<p><p>Precision Gears market is divided into various types, including Spur Gear, Helical Gear, Bevel Gear, Worm Gear, Gear Rack, and Others. Spur gears have straight teeth parallel to the axis and are suitable for applications requiring high speed and efficiency. Helical gears have angled teeth for smoother operation and higher load capacity. Bevel gears have conical teeth for changing direction of power transmission. Worm gears have a screw-like structure for high gear reduction. Gear racks are linear gears used with pinion gears for linear motion. Other types include planetary gears, internal gears, and crown gears.</p></p>
