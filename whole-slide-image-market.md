<p><p>Whole Slide Image Market can be divided into two main types based on imaging techniques: Brightfield and Fluorescence imaging. Brightfield imaging involves using a bright light source to illuminate samples, providing detailed and high-resolution images of tissue samples. On the other hand, Fluorescence imaging involves using fluorescent dyes to label specific structures in samples, allowing for visualization of specific molecules or proteins. Both types of imaging techniques have their own unique advantages and applications in the field of pathology and research.</p></p>
