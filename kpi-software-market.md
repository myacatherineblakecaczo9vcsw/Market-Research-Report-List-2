<p><p>KPI software is categorized into two main types based on deployment methods: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based KPI software is hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the software through the internet. This type of deployment offers flexibility and scalability, making it easy to implement and manage. On the other hand, on-premises KPI software is installed and maintained on the organization's own servers and infrastructure. This type of deployment provides control and security but may require more resources for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
